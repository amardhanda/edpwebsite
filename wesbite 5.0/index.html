Description of product

We are a group of students from Imperial College London and our aim is to
help the visually impaired explore new areas without the need of a guide.
Our solution is the Magic Map. Magic Map combines tactile and audio
feedback to allow the user to get a better understanding of any area without
the need of sight. We believe that Magic Map is user friendly, affordable and transportable.
